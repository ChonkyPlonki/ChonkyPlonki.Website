Vulpion
  • Project Type: Game
  • Game Engine: Unity Engine 2020.3.31f1
  • Status: Not Completed

Description

Vulpion was a curious exploration game I developed over 1.5 years. You played as a "Vulp", a fox-like person that dropped into the world "naked, compassionate and free".
I overscoped, unfortunately, and never finished the project - but I still love this game!

At the time I summarized the vision of the game as follows:

Press your feet into sun-kissed spores and ruffled lands. Open up to a psychologist and practice social skills to bond with others. Gift your heart to orphan animals and build homes for them. Bake bread, make tea and throw dinner parties as you thunder across the land. Show kindness, be vulnerable. Become a harbinger of goodness.
