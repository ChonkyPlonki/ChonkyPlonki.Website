Quick Info

  • Play on Itch: Steamlink
  • Project Type: Management Game (gamejam)
  • Role: Artist, Coder
  • Game Engine: Unity
Nonsensical Player Chores is a gamejam game I made together with five other super talented and cool people for the GMTKJam 2023.
All of us were spread across Sweden and England and created this game over 2 days remotely together.
We ended up scoring in the top 10 % of all teams who participated in GMTKJam which was really cool!
