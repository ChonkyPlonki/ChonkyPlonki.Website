Quick Info

  • Project Type: Management Game
  • Role: Coder, Artist
  • Game Engine: Unity
  • Status: Not Completed
WHISKER ISLAND is a project I worked on with two other awesome developers. We decided to work on the game for 3 months and work on it ca 1-4 hours a week each.
We set out to make a small and cute Factorio-inspired game, as we saw there weren't many factorio-like games (probably because they are very hard to make), and additionally, not Factorio-like games that were cute.
We ran into performance issues as it was hard to implement the transportation belts highly optimized (without working with Unity's DOTS system or Burst etc.).

Due to busy lives we ended up putting the project on indefinite hiatus after the 3 months, however I would love to revisit it again and make a very small game of it!
