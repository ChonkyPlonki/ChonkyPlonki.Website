VULPION is a curious exploration game I developed over 2 years (between 2019 and 2021). You play as a "Vulp", a fox-like person that dropped into the world "naked, compassionate and free". I overscoped and unfortunately never finished the project - but I love the project dearly!

I may yet pick it up again, but at the time I summarized the vision of the game as follows:

Press your feet into sun-kissed spores and ruffled lands. Open up to a psychologist and practice social skills to bond with others. Gift your heart to orphan animals and build homes for them. Bake bread, make tea and throw dinner parties as you thunder across the land. Show kindness, be vulnerable. Become a harbinger of goodness.
